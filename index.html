About Cirakas

More than a company, we're a tight-knit family that values work-life balance - enjoy chef-prepared lunches daily, team retreats, and a culture that encourages both risk-taking and relaxation. At Cirakas, we're constantly evolving with the tech landscape while staying true to our mission: building solutions that change lives.

Living in Full Spectrum

At Cirakas, our colours pulse with purpose—they're the unspoken manifesto of how we reinvent the ordinary. Every hue tells our story: of demo-driven teams where code speaks louder than titles, where "I don't know" opens doors to collective genius. This vibrant energy flows through our standups, electrifies our whiteboard sessions, and warms the spaces where breakthroughs happen over shared meals.

AI, Tech and more

We're a vibrant, young team of tech enthusiasts building cuttingedge solutions in healthcare and energy conservation. Our developers work with the full spectrum of modern web technologies - from JavaScript frameworks like React.js, Vue.js, AngularJS, and Next.js to powerful backend stacks using Node.js, Express.js, and NestJS. We craft beautiful, responsive interfaces with CSS3, SASS, Tailwind CSS, and Material UI, while leveraging TypeScript for robust code. Always innovating, we embrace NoCode platforms and AI technologies to accelerate development, using AI Agents to transform healthcare experiences for both clinicians and patients.
